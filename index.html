What is Beyond the Avatar?

In Beyond the Avatar, players step behind the bar and serve a diverse cast of guests â€” but the real challenge isn't just serving the drinks, it's reading the people. Each customer has a unique avatar, but who they really are goes beyond appearances.

Why this game?

  • Insight: Players gain personal insight into how they make decisions based on appearance, questions, and gut feeling.
  • Data Collection: Every player choice is stored as research data, offering a new way to study perception in virtual spaces.
  • Reflection: The game stimulates reflection on bias, digital identity, and first impressions.
  • Accessible & Fun: Easy to play, fun to experience, but still carrying a deeper message.
  • Educational & Research-Oriented: Offers a playful alternative to traditional surveys or video studies, making data collection more interactive and natural.
